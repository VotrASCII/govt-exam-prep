5kg LPG cylinder demand from immigrant labourers plummets up to 80% amid price surge

Demand for these portable gas cylinders creatively named after kids plunged after prices doubled following the West Asia war, at least four distributors said. These 5kg cylinders are primarily used by migrant workers and students, and can be bought off the shelf with just Aadhar as identity proof.
